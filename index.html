How is TeachMe different?

Have a look at the 3 main ways we're reinventing tutoring.

Registration

Whether you want to learn, need help on a subject or would like to teach one, Teachme offers a simplified way of signing up, for you to be able to learn or teach without any struggle.

Tutoring system

Find the best tutors for your needs easily thanks to our algorithm. You can then start to interact with them through messages, or book an in-person or online session on their in-app calendar.

Satisfacton philosophy

At TeachMe, we want you to be satisfied with our service. After a tutoring session, you will be able to leave a review on the teacher’s profile, and if you were not satisfied with the session we will pay you back!
